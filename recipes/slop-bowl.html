High-Protein Ground Turkey and Sweet Potato Slop Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • ¾ cup greek yogurt or sour cream
  • 2 Tbsp cooking oil (I like olive oil)
  • 1 Packet taco meat seasoning
  • Hot Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Sweet Potatoes

    Peel and dice your sweet potato into bite-sized cubes and put them into a large bowl. Drizzle with olive oil and add any seasonings you choose. Toss to cover the potatoes thoroughrly and put them in an air fryer at 400°F for 15-20 minutes, tossing them about every 10 minutes.

  2. Turkey Meat

    Warm up your pan or skillet to medium heat and add your ground turkey and seasoning packet. You may need to add some water or stock if the directions on the seasoning calls for it or if the meat looks too dry. Cook until the meat reaches an internal temprature of at least 165°F. I trust you.

  3. Assembly

    Once your meat is finished, put your potatoes into a soup bowl, put as much meat on top as you can fit (usually ⅓ to ½ of a pound). Dollop with greek yogurt or sour cream and finish it off with a dose of your favorite hot sauce.
